🛡️

100% Local Storage

Carbon Shadow does not use backend servers or databases to store your personal lifestyle footprint. All settings, preferences, and progress tracking are stored strictly in your browser's LocalStorage. Clearing your browser data will reset your Carbon Shadow.

ⓘ

Calculation Methodology

Estimates are calculated using EPA emission factors, IPCC lifecycle averages, and open-source environmental datasets. Base scores are modified live by your lifestyle preferences to simulate the dynamic nature of a real Carbon OS.
